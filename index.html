DEVELOPMENT OF A PUBLIC HEALTH CENTRE WORK PROGRAM MANAGEMENT SYSTEM WITH THE AGILE MODEL

Sirekap (Program Work Recapitulation Information System) is an information system designed to assist community health centers (Puskesmas) in managing the planning, implementation, and reporting of work programs in a structured and integrated manner. The system includes features for submitting activity proposals (KAK), recording accountability reports (LPJ), managing budgets, and monitoring activities—making it easier for staff to generate reports, track fund usage, and evaluate program effectiveness more efficiently and transparently.
